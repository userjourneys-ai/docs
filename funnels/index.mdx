<Note>
The Visual Funnel Builder is available on the **Pro plan**.
</Note>

The Visual Funnel Builder connects to your PostHog data to help you see where users drop off and understand why. Combined with [AI Interviews](/interviews/index), you get both the "what" and the "why" behind user behavior.

## Key Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Funnel Visualization" icon="filter">
    See conversion rates at each step of your user journeys.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI Drop-off Analysis" icon="magnifying-glass-chart">
    Automatically identify where and why users are leaving.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Session Replay Sync" icon="video">
    Watch replays of users who dropped off at specific steps.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cohort Analytics" icon="users">
    Track retention across different user segments.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect PostHog">
    Follow the [PostHog setup guide](/funnels/posthog) to connect your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Funnels sync automatically">
    Once connected, your funnels and session replays begin syncing immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Analyze drop-offs">
    View funnel visualizations and let AI identify friction points in your user journeys.
  </Step>
</Steps>
